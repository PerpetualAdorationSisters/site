Prayerfully consider partnering with the Adoration Sisters in their ministry dedicated to intercessory prayer.

Some opportunities include:

  • Pray with the Sisters in Adoration in the Christ the High Priest Chapel. There are many times available to pray in the real presence of Christ in the Blessed Sacrament.
  • Provide food and daily living supplies.
  • Volunteer your time at the Monastery:
    • Yard work and gardening
    • Teaching English
    • Assist with driving
  • Help with ongoing care and maintenance of the Monastery:
    • Contribute a cash gift
    • Make a non-cash gift (stocks, crops, other property)
    • Establish an Estate/Will pledge to the Adoration Sisters’ Endowment

To pray, donate food/supplies, or for volunteer opportunities please contact the Adoration Sisters at 605-336-2374 or email: adoratrices@sfcatholic.org.

Please contact the Catholic Community Foundation for Eastern South Dakota to discuss non-cash gifts and establishing an Estate/Will pledge for the Adoration Sisters’ Endowment at 888-246-3386 or 605-988-3796.

Your donation will make an impact!
