Mater Ecclesiae Monastery

The Perpetual Adoration Sisters of the Blessed Sacrament invite you to join them in their new home, Mater Ecclesiae Monastery. Visitors are welcome to attend Holy Mass, spend time in personal prayer or pray devotions in the chapel dedicated to Jesus the High Priest.

Visiting Hours:

Daily from 9:00am-12:00pm & 4:00pm-6:00pm

Gift Shop Hours:

Monday-Saturday 9:30am-5:30pm

Holy Mass:

Monday through Saturday at 7:30am

Please note that as a full-time priest chaplain is not available to serve the community, other parish responsibilities of the priests who minister to the monastery may cause slight changes to this schedule.

Adoration Chapel Hours:

Open to the public daily

7:00am-6:00pm

The Blessed Sacrament will be exposed in the monstrance each day as duties within the monastery allow.
